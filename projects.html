Epidemiological Dashboard for Monitoring and Modelling Malaria Incidence in Kenya

This interactive Power BI dashboard visualizes and models malaria incidence trends across Kenyan counties, providing valuable insights for public health monitoring and intervention planning. The dashboard integrates multi-year malaria data with climate indicators such as rainfall and temperature, allowing users to explore relationships between environmental factors and malaria prevalence. It also incorporates ITN (Insecticide-Treated Net) coverage rates to assess the effectiveness of preventive measures. Relevance: This project supports Kenya’s National Malaria Control Programme (NMCP) and aligns with SDG 3 – Good Health and Well-being, by promoting data-driven disease surveillance and policy decision-making. The model’s findings can guide resource allocation, prevention strategies, and improve public health outcomes in malaria-endemic regions like Kisumu, Kakamega, and Homa Bay
